## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [speakflow.com](https://speakflow.com)
2. Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner
3. Fill in your details:
   - Name
   - Email address
   - Password (min. 6 characters)
4. Accept the Terms of Service
5. Click **Create Account**
6. Verify your email address using the link sent to your inbox

### First-Time Onboarding

After registration, you'll go through a quick onboarding process:

1. **Tell us about yourself** - Set your name and preferred language
2. **Choose your level** - Select your current English proficiency
3. **Pick your interests** - Choose topics you want to learn about
4. **Set your goals** - Define what you want to achieve
5. **Configure preferences** - Set daily goals and notification preferences

### Logging In

1. Click **Login** in the top right corner
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Login**
4. (Optional) Check "Remember me" to stay logged in

## Learning Features

### Practice Sessions

#### Starting a Session

1. Click **Practice** in the navigation menu
2. Browse available lessons
3. Select a lesson based on:
   - Type (pronunciation, vocabulary, grammar, speaking)
   - Difficulty (beginner, intermediate, advanced)
   - Duration (5-30 minutes)
4. Click **Start Lesson**

#### Lesson Types

| Type | Description | XP Reward |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Pronunciation** | Practice speaking with AI feedback | 50 XP |
| **Vocabulary** | Learn new words and phrases | 50 XP |
| **Grammar** | Master grammar rules | 60 XP |
| **Speaking** | Practice conversations | 80 XP |
| **Listening** | Improve comprehension | 60 XP |
| **Comprehensive** | Mixed skill practice | 150 XP |

#### During a Lesson

- Read instructions carefully
- Complete each exercise
- For pronunciation: speak clearly into your microphone
- For multiple choice: select the correct answer
- For fill-in-blanks: type your answer
- Track your progress with the progress bar

#### Completing a Session

- Submit all answers
- Review your results
- Read feedback and suggestions
- Earn XP and unlock achievements

### AI Conversation Partner

Practice real conversations with our AI chatbot:

1. Navigate to **Practice → AI Chat**
2. Choose a topic or start a free conversation
3. Type or speak your responses
4. Get natural, contextual replies
5. Receive grammar and vocabulary suggestions

**Features:**
- Adjust difficulty level (beginner to advanced)
- Choose conversation topics (business, travel, daily life)
- Get pronunciation feedback on spoken responses

## Voice & Pronunciation

### Setting Up Your Microphone

1. Grant microphone access when prompted
2. Test your microphone in **Settings → Audio**
3. Adjust input volume if needed
4. Select your preferred microphone device

### Pronunciation Practice

1. Select a pronunciation lesson
2. Read the text displayed on screen
3. Click the microphone button and speak clearly
4. Wait for AI analysis
5. Review your score and feedback

### Understanding Feedback

| Score Range | Level | Meaning |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| 90-100% | Excellent | Near-native pronunciation |
| 75-89% | Good | Minor improvements needed |
| 60-74% | Fair | Noticeable accent, understandable |
| 0-59% | Needs Practice | Significant improvement needed |

### Feedback Details

- **Accuracy**: How correctly you pronounced each sound
- **Fluency**: How smoothly you spoke
- **Intonation**: Rise and fall of your voice
- **Pace**: Speed of your speech
- **Mispronounced Words**: Specific words to practice
- **Practice Tips**: Targeted exercises for improvement

### Text-to-Speech

Listen to correct pronunciation:

1. Click the speaker icon 🔊 next to any word or sentence
2. Choose voice preference (male/female, accent)
3. Adjust playback speed if needed
4. Repeat as many times as needed

## Vocabulary Learning

### Spaced Repetition System (SRS)

Our SRS algorithm optimizes vocabulary retention by scheduling reviews at optimal intervals.

**How it works:**
1. Learn new words
2. Review at increasing intervals (1 day, 3 days, 7 days, etc.)
3. Words you struggle with appear more frequently
4. Mastered words appear less often

### Vocabulary Dashboard

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Total Cards** | Number of words in your collection |
| **Due Today** | Words scheduled for review today |
| **Mastered** | Words you've fully learned |
| **Retention Rate** | Percentage of correct reviews |

### Adding New Words

**Manual Entry:**
1. Go to **Vocabulary → Add Word**
2. Enter the word and translation
3. Add an example sentence (optional)
4. Select difficulty level
5. Click **Save**

**From Lessons:**
- Words from lessons are automatically added
- Save words you encounter in AI conversations

### Reviewing Words

1. Click **Review Now** on the vocabulary dashboard
2. View the word and try to recall its meaning
3. Click **Show Answer** to reveal
4. Rate your recall:
   - **Again** (0) - Completely forgot
   - **Hard** (1) - Remembered with difficulty
   - **Good** (2) - Remembered correctly
   - **Easy** (3) - Very easy to remember

### Word Details

Each vocabulary entry includes:
- Word and translation
- Pronunciation guide
- Part of speech
- Definition
- Example sentence
- Synonyms and antonyms
- Audio pronunciation

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Beginners

1. **Start small** - 15 minutes daily is better than 2 hours weekly
2. **Use headphones** - Better audio quality for pronunciation
3. **Practice speaking aloud** - Don't just read silently
4. **Review mistakes** - Learn from incorrect answers
5. **Set a routine** - Practice at the same time each day

### For Intermediate Learners

1. **Challenge yourself** - Try higher difficulty lessons
2. **Use AI conversation** - Practice real dialogues
3. **Learn collocations** - Study word combinations
4. **Watch your intonation** - Pay attention to pitch and stress
5. **Join community** - Practice with other learners

### For Advanced Learners

1. **Focus on nuances** - Idioms, slang, cultural references
2. **Record yourself** - Compare with native speakers
3. **Discuss complex topics** - Politics, science, philosophy
4. **Write essays** - Get AI grammar feedback
5. **Teach others** - Best way to solidify knowledge

### Maximizing SRS Effectiveness

1. **Review daily** - Consistency is key
2. **Be honest with ratings** - Accurate ratings improve algorithm
3. **Don't skip hard words** - They need more practice
4. **Use example sentences** - Context helps retention
5. **Review before sleeping** - Better memory consolidation

### Building a Learning Habit

1. **Set achievable goals** - Start with 15 minutes
2. **Track your streak** - Don't break the chain
3. **Reward milestones** - Celebrate achievements
4. **Find an accountability partner** - Learn with friends
5. **Make it fun** - Enjoy the process
